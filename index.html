Make Your Events Royal with a Camel Ride

Camel ride on rent. You have definitely seen camels, especially in Rajasthan, and riding one is a joy for everyone. It’s interesting and fun. All children and adults enjoy a camel ride. Our camel is beautifully decorated from top to bottom.
The ride is very enjoyable. The handlers are friendly with the camel and make you feel comfortable by explaining everything. Camel rides are available in Delhi.
Our camels can go on long drives of almost 20 hours, covering over 900 km to arrive at your event. Perfect for birthday planners in Delhi.
Riding a camel is an experience in itself—just getting on is exciting! It’s like riding a horse, and kids absolutely love it. Camel rides usually last 25 minutes, and while fun, they can get tiring—so bring a light painkiller just in case.
